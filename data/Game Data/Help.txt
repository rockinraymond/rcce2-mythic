Chat Commands:
/pm <player name>, <message>: sends a private message to player (don't forget the comma!)
/p <message>: sends a message to your party
/yell <message>: sends a message to all players in game
/offer <player name>,<item name>,<amount>,<gold coins>: opens up trade dialog with specified player by offering a specified amount of an item for a specified amount of gold coins.
/invite <player name>: invites player to your party
/accept: accepts party invite
/leave: leaves current party
/allplayers: displays number of other players currently in game
/players: displays number of other players currently in zone
/me <message>: displays emote message(for role playing)
/saveme: teleports player to safety in the event of getting stuck(cannot be used in combat)
